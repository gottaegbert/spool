How it works

Three steps. Your data, your machine.

Everything is indexed locally. Your thinking stays where it belongs.

01

Pull your data with OpenCLI

OpenCLI pulls your bookmarks, stars, and saves from 50+ platforms to your machine. Spool indexes everything it captures.

02

Index everything

Spool watches ~/.claude/ and ~/.codex/ in real time. Every agent conversation, searchable the moment it's written.

03

Search and flow

A /spool skill in Claude Code. A spool CLI. Ask your agent to "build on last month's auth discussion" and it actually can.

Built for agents

Your coding agent is already the best
search engine you have.

Spool lets Claude Code, Codex, and any coding agent search your personal data — past sessions, bookmarks, stars, saves — from a single search box.

Every agent session, indexed automatically

Spool watches your session directories in real time. Every conversation you have with Claude Code or Codex becomes searchable the moment it's written. No manual export, no copy-paste.
